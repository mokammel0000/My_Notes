Legacy BIOS:
    -older,
    -entirely keyboard driven(ما ينفعش تتحكم فيه بالماوس),
    -usually simple in terms of UI
    -have either a black or blue-screen-of-death background color.

    
UEFI:
    -stands for Unified Extensible Firmware Interface, 
    -can be thought of as a newer type of BIOS, 
    -UEFI often includes graphics to show fan speed, temperature, and CPU clock speeds,
    -can sometimes be controlled with a mouse or trackpad.
