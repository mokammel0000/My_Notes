URL
    stands for [Uniform Resource Locator],
    it can be written in words or as an IP address.

    "academind.com/learn" is the (URL).
    "academind.com" is the (domain) of an website,
    "/learn" is the so-called (path),


you can use the main domain to visit the main website page, 
    also u can use the same domain followed by some predefined paths to visit more than one page.

In addition, you can visit most websites via "www.academind.com" or just "academind.com".
    Technically, "www" is a subdomain but most websites simply redirect traffic to "www" to the main page.
